After Pandemic Music Festival 2024 - Attractions FAQ

FAQ

Will there be a Snack bar in every corner of each stage?

Yes, there will be snack bars conveniently located in every corner of each stage.

Will there be Drinks Pubs for adults and children around each stage?

Yes, there will be Drinks Pubs catering to both adults and children around each stage.

On entrance, will each guest receive a free map of the event?

Yes, each guest will receive a free map of the event upon entrance.

Will every guest get a voucher for free drinks?

Yes, every guest will get a voucher to receive 2 free beers at Stage 1 and 1 free drink at Stage 2.

Will children receive free soft drinks?

Yes, children will receive free soft drinks between 17:00 and 19:00 within Stage 2 only.

Is there a soft play zone within Stage 2?

Yes, there will be a soft play zone within Stage 2.

Where will the shop zone be located?

The shop zone will be located at the entrance, just on the right-hand side, offering event lights, hats, t-shirts, and many more items.

What else can be found within the shops zone?

Within the shop zone, there will be pubs and bars, as well as additional surprises to discover.

Will there be a challenge for children?

Yes, there will be a challenge for children to find 12 letters around Stage 2. The first 10 kids to complete the challenge will win a backstage meeting with our event stars.

Will there be a lottery for parents?

Yes, there will be a lottery available for parents. Each ticket will cost £10, with the main prize being a brand new Audi S7, second place a 7-day holiday in Ibiza, and third place £10,000 in cash.

Where will the toilets be located?

Toilets will be located between the shopping zone and Stage 2 and will be free to use.
