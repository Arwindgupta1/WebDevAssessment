After Pandemic Music Festival 2024

FAQ

When and where will the festival take place?

The festival will take place on 20 June 2024 in Hoddesdon.

What is the lineup for the main stage?

  • Lady Gaga: 16:00 - 17:00
  • Zenek Martyniuk: 17:00 - 18:00
  • Britney Spears: 18:00 - 19:00
  • Metallica: 19:00 - 20:00
  • Boys: 20:00 - 21:00
  • Dj Omen: 21:00 - 23:00
  • Dj Kartofel: 23:00 - 01:00
  • Dj Base: 01:00 - 03:00

What is the lineup for Stage 2?

  • The Wiggles: 16:00 - 17:00
  • The Muppets: 17:00 - 18:00
  • The Kelly Family: 18:00 - 19:00
  • Kentish Gents: 19:00 - 20:00
  • Midsummer Knights: 20:00 - 21:00
  • Ultraglow: 21:00 - 23:00
  • Dj Bartheez: 23:00 - 01:00
  • David Guetta: 01:00 - 03:00

Where will the festival be held next year?

Next year, the festival will be held in Harlow.

Are there any age restrictions?

The festival is open to all ages, but those under 16 must be accompanied by an adult.

Can I bring my own food and drinks?

No, outside food and drinks are not allowed. There will be various food and drink vendors on site.
